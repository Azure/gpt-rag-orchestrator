## Negotiation Scenario: Consulting Engagement

**Service Type**: Strategic consulting, digital transformation, or process improvement
**Typical Engagement**: $200K - $1M over 3-12 months
**Team Structure**: Partner-led with senior consultants and analysts

### Key Value Propositions
- Deep industry expertise and proven methodologies
- Access to proprietary benchmarking data
- Track record of measurable business outcomes
- Knowledge transfer to internal teams
- C-suite relationships and change management support

### Buyer Context
- Organization facing significant strategic challenge or transformation
- Internal teams lack bandwidth or specialized expertise
- Board or executive team is pushing for external perspective
- Previous consulting engagements had mixed results
- Concerns about consultants who "just produce slides"

### Common Objections to Expect
1. **ROI Skepticism**: "How do we know this will actually deliver results?"
2. **Rate Concerns**: "Your day rates seem high compared to competitors."
3. **Scope Creep Fear**: "How do we prevent this from ballooning?"
4. **Knowledge Transfer**: "We don't want to become dependent on consultants."
5. **Team Quality**: "Will we get your A-team or junior staff?"

### Negotiation Levers
- Fixed-fee vs. time-and-materials pricing
- Success fees tied to outcomes
- Team composition and seniority mix
- Scope definition and change order process
- Intellectual property and deliverable ownership
- Ongoing retainer or advisory arrangement
